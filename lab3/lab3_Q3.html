Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • Cheese slices
  • Butter
  • Tomato slices
  • Bread slices

Instructions:

Place a skillet on medium heat. Cook bacon until crispy. Remove bacon and drain excess grease.

Butter one side of each bread slice. On the non-buttered side, layer cheese, bacon, and tomato slices. Top with another bread slice, buttered side out.

Place the sandwich in the skillet and cook until golden brown on each side and the cheese is melted.

Serve hot and enjoy your delicious grilled cheese sandwich with bacon and tomato!
